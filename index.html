Ella Valentine
Ella Bunker is an artist living in Boorloo/Perth. Their practice consists of painting and sculpture works with agar jelly, wire, dough, cake, and found objects. Ella's work is intrinsically linked to their mental illness, with its formal qualities often derived from the visual phenomena they experience. Their work concerns embodiment, attempting to reconcile the mess and rot of the physical world with the absolute domain of the mental; impressions are realised playfully, but with the transience of their organic material always foregrounded.
